## What you are building

A complete HTML wireframe for the HYSN DOOH & hyads page (`/dooh-hyads`).

This page serves two completely different audiences. They land here for opposite reasons. The page must speak to both clearly, with separate paths. Think first, then build.

---

## Think before you build

1. **Two audiences land here:**
   - **Audience A — Werbetreibende (advertisers):** Local businesses, regional brands, lokale Händler who want to advertise on screens. They know traditional advertising (Plakat, Print, Social Media) but are exploring DOOH for the first time. They're price-sensitive and want to understand reach, locations, cost, and how to actually book a campaign.
   - **Audience B — Location partners:** Property owners, supermarket operators, building managers, municipalities, or businesses with high-traffic locations who could host a screen. They are nearly unaware that their footfall is monetizable. They want to understand passive income, zero-effort operation, and revenue model.

2. **What does each audience need to think to inquire?**
   - **Werbetreibende:** "These screens reach my exact local customers. The price is reasonable. I can launch quickly. The targeting is real — not just spray and pray."
   - **Location partners:** "This generates real income with zero effort on my side. HYSN handles everything. The advertising won't damage my brand."

3. **What would actually make each audience inquire?**
   - **Werbetreibende:** Visible locations they recognize. Realistic pricing structure. Quick start path. Audience targeting clarity. Content production support.
   - **Location partners:** Realistic monthly revenue numbers. Revenue model explained. Effort = zero clarity. Advertising standards reassurance. Risk-free start path (e.g. pilot with one screen).

4. **What would stop each audience?**
   - **Werbetreibende:** Vague reach claims. No visible locations. Hidden pricing. Long sales cycle promised.
   - **Location partners:** Skeptical about realistic income. Worry that advertising looks cheap. Concern about contract length. Fear of operational complexity.

Now design the page that answers (3) and removes (4) for both audiences clearly separated.

## Depth instructions

### Werbetreibende advantages
Reason through what's actually different about DOOH vs traditional local advertising:

1. **Local precision:** Unlike a plakat that reaches everyone in the city, a hyads screen at a specific REWE in Frankfurt reaches the people actually shopping there. More relevant audience, less waste.
2. **Real physical visibility:** Unlike Instagram or Google ads that disappear after 3 seconds, a screen runs continuously throughout the day. People see it multiple times in their normal routine.
3. **Faster turnaround:** Print plakat campaigns require weeks of design, printing, posting. A digital campaign can launch in days. Content can be updated mid-campaign if needed.

Write each as a feature block with a 2–3 sentence explanation that lands for someone evaluating their advertising options.

### Standort-Partner use cases
Reason through who benefits and how:

- **Supermarket entrance:** 800–2000 customers per day, screen at entrance shows local advertisers. Realistic monthly revenue for the location. Operational effort: zero — HYSN's content schedule handles everything.
- **Fitness studio reception:** 150–400 visitors per day, captive audience while waiting. Different advertiser mix (fitness products, local clinics, sports nutrition).
- **Tankstelle:** 500–1500 visitors per day, very short attention. Screen at pump or near kasse. Local advertisers (restaurants, services) benefit from the captive 2-minute window.
- **Bürogebäude-Lobby:** 200–800 office workers per day. Higher-end advertiser mix (financial services, business lunches, executive products).

Each as a card showing what kind of partner fits and what realistic revenue context looks like.

### Revenue model — the example calculation
Format something like:

"Ein Standort mit ca. 1.000 Besuchern pro Tag und 4 aktiven Werbepartnern generiert in der Regel zwischen 250 € und 450 € pro Monat. Die genaue Höhe hängt von Standortqualität, Werbeumfeld und Kampagnenvolumen ab — diese Werte dienen zur Orientierung, nicht als Garantie."

Adjust numbers to be realistic for HYSN's actual operating context. Mark clearly as orientation.

### FAQ split between the two audiences

**Werbetreibende-Fragen (3):**
- "Wie viele Personen sehen meine Werbung pro Monat?"
- "Was kostet eine Kampagne ungefähr?"
- "Wie schnell kann ich starten?"

**Standort-Partner-Fragen (3):**
- "Wie hoch ist meine realistische monatliche Einnahme?"
- "Was muss ich selbst tun?"
- "Was passiert, wenn ich aussteigen will?"

Real answers, honest, no overpromise.

## Wireframe style rules — non-negotiable

- Single self-contained HTML file. No external dependencies.
- Grayscale palette: `#ffffff`, `#f4f4f4`, `#d0d0d0`, `#333333`, `#111111`
- Body `#f0f0f0`, page wrapper `#ffffff`, max-width 900px centered, 250px right margin
- Font: `system-ui, -apple-system, sans-serif`
- Section labels: `[SECTION NAME]` 10px uppercase gray
- The two audience sections must be visually distinct (e.g. one with #fafafa background, one with #f0f0f0 background) so the dual-audience structure is immediately readable
- Image placeholders: gray rectangle with diagonal SVG cross + German caption
- Buttons: 2px solid #333, no fill, padding 12px 28px
- Annotations: sticky-note boxes right margin, every section gets one
- Sections separated by 1px #d0d0d0 border
- Desktop only
